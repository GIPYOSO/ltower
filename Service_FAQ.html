FAQ

  • Q

    I'm going to use the observatory before going to the airport. Is there a place to store my luggage?    keyboard_arrow_down
    A

    Personal luggage larger than the appropriate standard size is restricted upon entry.

    There is no luggage storage inside the observatory, so please use the luggage storage at Lotte World Mall if necessary. (The nearest store is next to the B1F GAP store and next to the The Body Shop store.)

  • Q

    I would like to purchase group tickets in advance.    keyboard_arrow_down
    A

    Group rates apply from 15 people or more, but for more information, please contact the group ticket office.

  • Q

    I lost something. Who should I contact?    keyboard_arrow_down
    A

    Lost and found collection and storage is managed by the on-site office. In case of lost property, please notify the information on the 1st basement and 117th floors.

  • Q

    Can I get a discount if I have not been able to bring proof of documents such as infants, children of national merit, or the disabled?    keyboard_arrow_down
    A

    In principle, fare discounts are based on supporting documents, so if you do not bring them, you cannot receive a discount.

  • Q

    How can I dine in the 123rd floor lounge?    keyboard_arrow_down
    A

    The 123 Lounge can be accessed by using the lounge elevator on the 121st floor.

    However, if a specific event is scheduled or if meals are restricted due to seat and space restrictions, access to the lounge may be temporarily suspende without prior notice.

  • Q

    Is there a place to enjoy a drink?    keyboard_arrow_down
    A

    At 119F Character Dessert Cafe, you can enjoy slushies and drinks, including Sky Friends' friend Rota ice cream.

    At the 122F Seoul Sky Cafe, you can find drinks and desserts that can only be found in Seoul Sky, such as coffee, sky juice, and cloud cake.

  • Q

    I want to change the date due to bad weather.    keyboard_arrow_down
    A

    To change the date and time after online or on-site issuance, the same rules as cancellation/refund apply and a certain fee will be incurred.

    Therefore, please select the date and time carefully when making the first reservation.

    At the Seoul Sky Observatory, you can enjoy all the contents of the observatory the same regardless of the weather, such as sunny, cloudy, or rainy days.

    (Use of some facilities may be restricted without prior notice for operational reasons.)

  • Q

    I would like to purchase an observatory commemorative product.    keyboard_arrow_down
    A

    There is a gift shop on the 121st floor and at the exit of the observatory on the 1st basement floor.

    We sell various products with motifs of SEOUL SKY and the observatory character SKY FRIENDS.

    You can purchase products by visiting the product store on the 1st basement floor without going up the observation floor.

  • Q

    I would like to keep a commemorative photo of my visit.    keyboard_arrow_down
    A

    There are a total of 4 photo zones in Seoul Sky.

    You can meet two welcome photos on the first basement floor, the waiting floor, Auto Capture on the 117th floor, and the photo zone on the 118F Skydeck.

    After visiting the observatory, visit the photo shop located on the exit road 120F and B1F, and you can meet all the photos taken in the observatory as your own album.

    However, all shootings can be recognized by the QR code on the admission ticket or the photo ticket provided at the time of shooting, so do not forget it until you leave.

  • Q

    Can I re-enter the observatory with the same ticket?    keyboard_arrow_down
    A

    All tickets, including paper tickets and mobile tickets, can only be entered once.

  • Q

    I got a ticket and lost it before entering the observatory. Can I get a replacement?    keyboard_arrow_down
    A

    Observation deck tickets cannot be reissued if lost. Please be mindful of ticket management. In particular, please keep mobile tickets carefully to avoid data deletion or screen damage.
