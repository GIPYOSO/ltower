Group information

Reservation information

Group rates apply for groups of 15 or more.

For more information on rates and groups, please contact the person in charge.

- Person in charge (groups and corporations) Domestic and corporate Overseas

Instructions for use

With the ticket issued, you can use the Lotte World Tower Observatory Seoul Sky (Underground Lobby Level, 117F ~ 122F).

Admission to the observatory is limited to the date printed (marked) on the ticket issued, and admission is restricted on other dates.

Information on the location of the group ticket office

Group bus drop off area and parking lot information

After entering the Jamsil-gil underground road, proceed to the right in the middle of the road, go straight, make a U-turn, and enter the road on the right.

After getting off the group, park using the Lotte World Adventure group bus parking lot.
