118FSky Deck

Feel as if you are walking through the sky from the sky deck, which has been recorded as the 'highest glass-floor observation deck' in the Guinness World Records.

120F Sky Terrace

Enjoy the refreshing and open view of Seoul from the outdoor terrace on the 120th floor at a height of 486m with the cool outside air.

Sky Shuttle

Teleport from the 1st basement floor to the 118th floor in just 1 minute!

For the first time in the world, monitor screens are installed on the ceiling and three sides of the wall, providing an immersive feeling as if you are riding in VR.

Media Table

It provides digital content that allows you to see the current appearance of Seoul as seen from the observatory as well as the Joseon Dynasty reproduced in a 3D environment.

You can view the information you want with a simple touch of the screen.

Keep your special memories in Seoul Sky photos.

Location: B1F, 117F, 118F, 120F Where to find: 120F, B1F (Exitjang-ro)
