Sky Bridge Tour Reservation Information

• Tour tickets can be reserved one month prior to the date of use, and the reservation period may be adjusted.

* Only same-day tour tickets can be purchased at the on-site ticket office.

• Tickets can be purchased online, through the mobile application, and at the on-site ticket office (B1 ticket office, 117th floor tour information desk).
